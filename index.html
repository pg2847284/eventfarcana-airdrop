What is the $FAR Token?
The $FAR* token is the utility token of the Farcana game and ecosystem. It not only allows seamless transactions but also has a number of other uses within the Farcana ecosystem. It will enable gamers to buy and access:
‍
• Season passes with cosmetic rewards
• Tournament Passes (T-Passes)
• Tournament Entry (T-Entry)
• Premium NFT Skins
• Crafting Particles
• In-game credits
•  Unique Pets
• And more!
